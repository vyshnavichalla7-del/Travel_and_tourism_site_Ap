History

The Sri Sri Sri Trimukha Vigneswara Panchayathana Devalayam is a well-known temple located near Narasimhanagar, Satya Sai Nagar, Ganesh Colony in Visakhapatnam. It is a Panchayatana style temple, meaning it typically houses five deities, with Lord Ganesha (Vigneshwara) as the central deity. This temple is revered by devotees for its spiritual significance and regular religious events. It has a good reputation in the local community and is easily accessible within the city.
