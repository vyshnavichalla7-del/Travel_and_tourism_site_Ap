Sri Kamakshi Vaidyanatha

History

  • The temple is located in Kotluru, Andhra Pradesh, and is called Sri Kamakshi Vaidyanatha Swamy Temple.
  • The presiding deities are Lord Vaidyanatha Swamy (a form of Shiva) and Goddess Kamakshi.
  • Architecturally, it is considered very old — one source claims it dates to the 10th century.
  • It is situated on the banks of the River Penna.
  • There is also a Sri Chakram installed in the Kamakshi temple in this complex, attributed to Sankaracharya, which adds to its spiritual significance
