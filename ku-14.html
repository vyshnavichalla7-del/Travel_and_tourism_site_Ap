Shivaji Maharaj Temple

History

The Shivaji Maharaj Temple, also known as Sree Shivaji Spoorthy Kendram, is a memorial dedicated to Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj, the legendary Maratha king. Located in Srisailam, Andhra Pradesh, the temple commemorates Shivaji Maharaj's visit to the region. The complex was inaugurated in 2004, featuring a bronze statue of Shivaji Maharaj and murals depicting significant events from his life.
