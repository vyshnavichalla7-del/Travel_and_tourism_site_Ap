Padmanabha Swamy Temple

History

  • Inscriptions & Stone Carvings — See if there are any inscriptions in Telugu, Sanskrit etc. on temple walls or pillars; these often record donor names, dates, dynasty, renovations.
  • Local Oral Tradition / Elders — Talking to priests or village elders might reveal legends about who built it, when, and which dynasty supported it.
  • Architectural Style — The style of the temple (Dravidian gopuram, material used, layout of mandapam, sanctum etc.) can give clues about period (for example, Vijayanagara, Chola, later).
  • Records in District Gazetteer / Archaeology Dept. — The Kadapa district or Andhra Pradesh state archives may have records on temple land grants, endowments.
