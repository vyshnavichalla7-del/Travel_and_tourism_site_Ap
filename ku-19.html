Konda Reddy Fort

History

  • Also known as Kondareddy Buruju, this fort is located in the heart of Kurnool city, Andhra Pradesh.
  • It's a standalone watchtower and is the only visible remnant of what was once a larger fort complex.
  • The structure is made of stone blocks, with a circular bastion and underground passages (some now closed for safety).
  • The fort offers a panoramic view of the city and is often visited for its historical ambience and photography
