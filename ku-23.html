Belum Caves

History

  • Ancient habitation: Archaeological evidence shows that Jain and Buddhist monks used the caves for meditation around 2000 years ago.
  • Artifacts like clay vessels and tools were discovered during excavations in the 1980s.
  • There's a section named "Meditation Hall", where monks are believed to have meditated in silence. It remains a quiet and spiritual part of the cave.
