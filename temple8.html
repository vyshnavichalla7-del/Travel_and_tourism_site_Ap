History

The Sri Sri Sri Bhuneela Sametha Vaibhava Venkateswara Swamy Temple is located in Madhurawada, Visakhapatnam. It is a well-regarded temple dedicated to Lord Venkateswara along with Bhudevi and Sridevi (consorts of the Lord). The temple is popular among devotees in the region for its spiritual ambiance and regular religious activities. It has a good rating from visitors and is known for celebrating traditional festivals with devotion. The temple is part of the vibrant religious landscape of Visakhapatnam, attracting locals and visitors alike for worship and events.
