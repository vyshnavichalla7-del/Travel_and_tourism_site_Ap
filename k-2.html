Sri Gandi Veeranjaneya Swamy

History

  • The origins of Gandi Kshetram are deeply rooted in the Ramayana.
  • It is believed that during Lord Rama’s return journey from Lanka to Ayodhya (after defeating Ravana), he passed through this area.
  • Here, he rested for a while by the banks of the Papagni River.
