Nemalla Park

History

  • Establishment: In 2008, 67 peacocks and peahens were relocated from the Nehru Zoological Park in Hyderabad to Idupulapaya. The Andhra Pradesh Forest Department was entrusted with the management and conservation efforts.
  • Facilities: The sanctuary comprises several enclosures, including separate sections for adult peacocks and chicks. It also features a nursery managed by the forest department to support breeding activities.
  • Ecological Significance: Situated amidst scrub forests with tree plantations, the sanctuary provides a natural habitat conducive to the well-being and reproduction of peafowls.
