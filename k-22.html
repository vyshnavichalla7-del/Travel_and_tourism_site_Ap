Shilparamam

History

  • Shilparamam Pulivendula is a cultural and handicraft village located in Pulivendula, a town in Kadapa district, Andhra Pradesh.
  • It is part of the Shilparamam initiative, a series of cultural complexes established across various towns in Andhra Pradesh to promote traditional arts, crafts, and cultural heritage
