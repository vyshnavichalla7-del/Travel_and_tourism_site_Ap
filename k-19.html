YSR Ghat

History

  • Following his untimely death, the Andhra Pradesh government established YSR Ghat at his native village, Idupulapaya, to honor his legacy and contributions to the state's development. The memorial serves as a place for public homage and reflection, attracting thousands of visitors annually, especially on the anniversary of his death.
