Nagarjuna Sagar Dam

History

  • Nagarjuna Sagar is a large multipurpose dam built across the Krishna River.
  • Construction began in the mid‑1950s, shortly after India’s independence, as part of efforts to improve irrigation, generate power, and hold water for dry seasons.
  • The project was conceived as one of the early big infrastructure works aimed at transforming agriculture and elevating living standards in this region.
