Potuluri Veerabrahmendra Swamy Temple

History

  • Potuluri Veerabrahmendra Swamy (also called “Brahmamgari” / Veera Brahmam) was a saint and prophet in Andhra Pradesh.
  • He is famous for Kalagnanam — his predictions or sayings about the future.
  • He was born around 1610 CE.
  • He entered Jeeva Samadhi (i.e. took samadhi alive, a spiritually significant event) in 1693 CE.
  • After his samadhi, a “Matham” (monastery / religious place) was constructed at the site of his Jeeva Samadhi in Kandimallayapalle.
  • On the walls of the Matham, his preachings and predictions are inscribed.
  • It is a pilgrimage center. Devotees come from various nearby states.
