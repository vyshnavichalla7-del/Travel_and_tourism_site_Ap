Taj Mahal

History

  • The temple is known as Sri Lakshmi Venkateswara Swamy Temple, also called Devuni Kadapa, Devuni Gadapa (“Devuni” = of God; “Gadapa” = entrance/threshold). Located in Kadapa city, Andhra Pradesh
  • It is called Tirumala Tholi Gadapa — meaning the first entrance/threshold to Lord Venkateswara for pilgrims visiting Tirumala. Devotees traditionally visit here before going to Tirumala
  • The idol of Lord Venkateswara is said to have been installed by Kripacharya (a character from Mahabharata). In Puranas this place is mentioned as “Kripavathi Kshetram
  • Built in Vijayanagara style architecture. Temple improvements by Vijayanagara kings and local rulers. Tallapaka Annamacharya (the composer), heads of Ahobilam mutt, Mahakavi Kshetrayya etc. have visited.
