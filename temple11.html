History

The Kanyaka Parameshwari Temple in Anakapalli is a well-known temple dedicated to Goddess Kanyaka Parameshwari, a revered form of the Mother Goddess. The temple is part of the rich religious and cultural heritage of Anakapalli and sees a large number of devotees, especially during festivals and special occasions. The temple is known for its vibrant celebrations and spiritual atmosphere, attracting devotees from nearby areas. The temple is located in Anakapalli town, and it is often mentioned together with other major temples like Nookambika Temple and Satyanarayana Temple, forming an important spiritual circuit for pilgrims in the region.
