Nallamala Hills

History

  • The Nallamala Hills are part of the Eastern Ghats, a very old mountain/forest range in India.
  • These hills stretch almost north‑to‑south, roughly 430 km in length, between the Krishna and Penna rivers.
  • The range forms the eastern boundary of the Rayalaseema region in Andhra Pradesh, and also extends into parts of Telangana.
