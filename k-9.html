Sri Sri Sri Prasanna Visveshwar Temple

History

  • There is a temple called Sri Sri Sri Prasanna Visveshwara Swamy Temple, located on YV Street, Ganagapeta, Kadapa, Andhra Pradesh.
  • According to a travel/temples‑blog list, it is one of the older Shiva temples in Kadapa — history said to span about 470 years.
  • The sanctum is said to house a Shivalinga, flanked by Lord Ganesha and Goddess Annapurna (on respective sides) in that temple.
  • Timings listed in that source: 6:00 AM – 7:00 PM daily.
