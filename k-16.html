Choudeswari Temple

History

  • The temple is dedicated to Goddess Chowdeswari (Chowdeshwari / Choudeswari Devi).
  • It’s considered a Devi temple of importance in Kadapa, visited especially during certain festivals (like Dasara).
  • It’s often described by devotees as an older temple, although no specific date is reliably attested.
