Pathalaganga

History

  • Pathala Ganga is a sacred site located near the Srisailam Mallikarjuna Temple in Andhra Pradesh. Situated in the backwaters of the Krishna River, it is considered an auspicious place for devotees to take a holy dip. The site is approximately 1 km from the temple, accessible via a 500-step pathway or a ropeway.
  • The stepped pathway was constructed by King Prolaya Vema Reddy in the 14th century.
