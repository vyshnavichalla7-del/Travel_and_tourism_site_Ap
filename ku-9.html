Sakshi Ganpati Temple

History

According to local beliefs, a visit to Srisailam is considered incomplete if one does not first offer prayers at the Sakshi Ganapati Temple. Devotees believe that Ganesha records their visit here, ensuring that their pilgrimage to the main temple is duly noted in the divine records. The temple is also associated with the Skanda Purana, which mentions Ganesha's role in documenting the deeds of devotees.
