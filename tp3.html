munjuluru waterfalls

overview

Munjuluru Waterfalls is a scenic natural attraction located in the West Godavari district of Andhra Pradesh, India, near the town of Kannapuram and about 40 km from Jangareddigudem. The waterfall is known for its serene beauty, surrounded by lush greenery and rocky terrain, making it a peaceful spot for nature lovers and picnickers. The area offers trekking opportunities with views of diverse flora and fauna. The best time to visit is during the monsoon season (June to September) when the falls are at their fullest. It is free to enter, and the falls are open from 8 am to 5 pm. The nearest railway station is Eluru, and there is no entry fee for visitors. The region is rich in biodiversity and provides a tranquil escape into nature's beauty.
