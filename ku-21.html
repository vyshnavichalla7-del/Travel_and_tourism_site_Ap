Shikar Darshanam

History

  • The word “Shikhar” means peak, and “Darshan” means vision or sight of the divine.
  • In ancient scriptures, it is said that a mere glimpse of the Srisailam Shikharam is equal to having darshan of the deity inside the main temple.
  • It is also one of the Ashta Dwaras (eight gateways) of Srisaila Kshetram, and is known as the Shikhar Dwara.
  • A stone inscription there says: “Antaralayam darshanam vina Shikharam darshanam punyam” → Even if one cannot see the inner sanctum, seeing the Shikhar is considered highly meritorious
